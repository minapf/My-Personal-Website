About

Welcome to my personal website! I’m an architect and designer with over 5 years of experience working on residential and diverse building projects. My passion for creating thoughtful, functional, and visually engaging spaces has shaped my journey in architecture. Now, I’m exploring a new path—transitioning into front-end development—where I aim to combine my design sensibilities with interactive digital experiences. This site showcases a collection of my architectural work and serves as my first step into the world of web development. Thanks for visiting!
